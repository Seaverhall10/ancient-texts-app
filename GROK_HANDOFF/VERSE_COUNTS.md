## IMPORTANT: Versification Notes

- These counts are from the **Hebrew Masoretic Text** (BHS/WLC) via OSHB.
- Some English Bibles (KJV, NASB, ESV) may have DIFFERENT verse numbering for certain books.
- **Psalms**: Hebrew verse numbering includes superscriptions as verse 1, so Psalm 3 has 9 verses (not 8). Use OUR numbers.
- **Daniel**: Chapters 3-4 have different verse counts in Hebrew vs. English translations (Hebrew Daniel 3 = 33 verses including what English Bibles call "3:31-33" as "4:1-3"). Use OUR numbers.
