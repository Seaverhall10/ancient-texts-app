If the slave chooses permanent servitude (loving his master, wife, and children), he forfeits chofshi status through an ear-piercing ritual (Ex 21:5-6). The term also describes freedom from obligation or liability (1 Sam 17:25). In Job 3:19, the slave is chofshi from his master in death, suggesting freedom as release from earthly constraints.
