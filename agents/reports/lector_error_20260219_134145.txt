        "definition": "Verb meaning to dwell, settle, or abide, emphasizing permanent or semi-permanent residence. From this root derives mishkan (tabernacle), YHWH's dwelling place among Israel. Shakan describes both human settlement (Genesis 9:27, 'may God enlarge Japheth, and may he shakan in the tents of Shem') and divine indwelling. Exodus 25:8 commands building the sanctuary 'that I may shakan among them,' establishing the tabernacle as locus of divine presence.
