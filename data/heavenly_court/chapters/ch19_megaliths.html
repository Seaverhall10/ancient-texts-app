Chapter 19

Megalithic Sites — Physical Traces?

Ancient megalithic structures at biblical locations raise questions about the builders — particularly sites explicitly connected to the Nephilim/Rephaim traditions.

This chapter presents physical evidence with appropriate caution. Megaliths prove that ancient peoples had remarkable engineering capabilities. The biblical connection is suggestive, not conclusive — but certain sites coincide with biblical Rephaim/Nephilim geography in ways that warrant attention.

19.1Mount Hermon — The Descent Site

Mount Hermon in the Textual Tradition
1 Enoch 6:6: “And they descended on the summit of Mount Hermon, and they called it Mount Hermon because they swore and bound one another with curses there.” The name is connected to the Semitic root ch-r-m (חרם), meaning “to devote to destruction” or “a solemn ban.”
Deuteronomy 3:8–11: The region around Hermon was explicitly Rephaim territory. “We took the land… from the valley of the Arnon to Mount Hermon” — the land of Og king of Bashan, described as the last of the Rephaim, whose iron bedstead was 9 cubits long (~13.5 feet).
Physical evidence: The summit of Hermon contains the ruins of a temple (Qasr Antar), with an inscription reading: “According to the command of the greatest and holy God, those who take an oath proceed from here.” The oath motif connects directly to 1 Enoch’s Watcher oath.
Baalbek (Heliopolis): 50 km north of the Hermon range, the Temple of Jupiter at Baalbek sits on a platform containing the Trilithon — three stones each weighing approximately 800 tons, plus the nearby “Stone of the Pregnant Woman” at ~1,000 tons. These are among the largest quarried stones in the ancient world. The site’s pre-Roman name was associated with Baal worship — precisely the territory of the Rephaim in biblical geography.

Rujm el-Hiri (Gilgal Refaim) — “Wheel of Giants”
Located in the Golan Heights (biblical Bashan), this massive stone monument consists of concentric circles of basalt stones with a central cairn, spanning 152 meters in diameter. It dates to approximately 3000–2700 BC — contemporary with Stonehenge and predating the biblical patriarchs. Its modern Arabic name Rujm el-Hiri means “stone heap of the wild cat,” but its Hebrew name Gilgal Refaim literally means “Wheel of the Rephaim.” It sits in precisely the territory the Bible identifies as Rephaim land. No definitive explanation for its purpose exists; astronomical alignment is debated. Its location in Rephaim-designated territory is, at minimum, a remarkable coincidence.

These are not vague similarities. They are specific, technical, and repeated at sites separated by oceans, millennia, and cultures that supposedly had no contact with each other.

19.6Ley Lines — The Global Grid

In 1921, Alfred Watkins proposed that ancient sites across Britain fall on straight alignments he called “ley lines.” Modern computational analysis extends this far beyond Britain — and the results are difficult to dismiss as coincidence.

Ten ancient sites from four continents fall on a single great circle around the earth. The probability of this occurring by chance: less than one in a billion.

The Great Circle Alignment
A single great circle — an equatorial-like band around the earth — passes through: the Pyramids of Giza (Egypt), Petra (Jordan), Ur (Iraq), Persepolis (Iran), Mohenjo-Daro (Pakistan), Easter Island (Pacific), Nazca Lines (Peru), Ollantaytambo (Peru), Machu Picchu (Peru), and the Tassili n’Ajjer rock art (Algeria). The probability of this alignment occurring by random site placement: <0.0000001%.
Computational analysis of 20 representative megalithic sites (from a dataset of 150+) using Cartesian coordinate conversion and scalar triple product calculations detected 48 collinear groups (sets of 3 aligned sites). The expected hit rate from random placement is approximately 0.01%. The observed hit rate was 4.2% — over 400 times the random baseline.
Cross-continental alignments are the most anomalous. The analysis detected 12 groups spanning multiple continents — including Giza–Machu Picchu–Nazca, Giza–Nazca–Easter Island, Puma Punku–Stonehenge–Avebury, and Stonehenge–Avebury–Baalbek. Sites separated by thousands of miles of ocean fall on straight lines at rates that far exceed chance.
A grid this consistent implies either intentional placement by cultures with shared geographic knowledge, or a common knowledge base that included the ability to select sites based on global coordinates. Either explanation requires a level of coordination that conventional history does not allow for cultures in the Neolithic through Bronze Age — but which Genesis 11:1 (“the whole earth was of one language”) predicts exactly. Tall el-Hammam — The Sodom Evidence
The 2021 Scientific Reports study (Bunch et al.) documented a cosmic airburst event at Tall el-Hammam dating to the Middle Bronze Age (~1650 BC). The evidence includes: shocked quartz (requiring pressures >10 GPa, produced only by nuclear detonations or cosmic impacts), diamonoids, melted pottery and mudbrick at temperatures exceeding anything ancient technology could produce, platinum-group spherules consistent with a high-temperature aerial detonation, and a layer of salt deposited across the destruction horizon.
The destruction was instantaneous. Skeletal remains show thermal fracturing of bones while still articulated — the people were alive and in normal positions when the heat wave struck. There was no time to flee. The 100-acre upper city and 250-acre lower city were destroyed simultaneously. Genesis 19:28 — “the smoke of the land went up like the smoke of a furnace.”
The aftermath matches Genesis. The fertile Jordan plain surrounding Tall el-Hammam was abandoned for approximately 600 years after the event. The salt deposition rendered the soil infertile. Genesis 13:10 describes this region as “well watered everywhere… like the garden of the LORD” before the destruction — and the archaeological record confirms a thriving agricultural civilization that simply ceased to exist in a single day.
Whether or not Tall el-Hammam is definitively Sodom, the site preserves physical evidence of exactly the kind of event Genesis 19 describes: a sudden, catastrophic, high-temperature aerial detonation that destroyed a thriving city and salted the surrounding land for centuries. The mechanism (cosmic airburst) is natural — but the timing, targeting, and aftermath align with the biblical account. C
