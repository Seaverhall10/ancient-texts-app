Part III

The Ancient Witnesses

In 1947, a shepherd’s thrown stone broke open 2,000 years of silence. The Dead Sea Scrolls confirmed the biblical text’s integrity and revealed that the Second Temple literature — 1 Enoch, the Book of Giants, Jubilees — was not fringe speculation but mainstream Jewish reading, copied by the same scribes who preserved Isaiah. This section begins with the physical evidence and then examines the texts it preserved.
