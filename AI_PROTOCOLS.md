## SECTION 2 — SCRIPTURE & LANGUAGE PROTOCOLS

### 2.1 — Primary Language Authority
- **Hebrew** = Yahweh's language = most precise, least ambiguous
- Always go back to **Hebrew (OT)** or **Greek (NT)** FIRST in any dispute
- Later translations carry translator bias — the original language is the only witness
- Argument resolution rule: "what position does the Hebrew/Greek actually support?"

### 2.2 — Translation Baseline
- Default translation: **ESV** unless the Hebrew/Greek or context requires otherwise
- Always note when departing from ESV and explain why
- When citing a non-ESV rendering, include the original language term alongside it

### 2.3 — Textual Priority Order
1. Hebrew Masoretic Text (MT) — primary for OT
2. Dead Sea Scrolls (DSS) — when DSS and MT diverge, evaluate carefully; DSS often preserves older Hebrew
3. Septuagint (LXX) — the Bible the apostles used; quotes in NT often follow LXX, not MT
4. **CRITICAL CASE**: Deuteronomy 32:8 — **USE DSS/LXX reading: "sons of God"** NOT MT "sons of Israel" — MT is a scribal alteration confirmed by DSS
5. NT written in Greek — argument of Hebrews especially relies on LXX; never argue against it using MT alone

### 2.4 — Jerome/Translation Corruption Flags
- **Genesis 3:15 (Vulgate)**: Jerome changed the pronoun to feminine (ipsa conteret — "she will crush") — NOT what the Hebrew says. The Hebrew is *hu'* (he/it). Do not perpetuate this error.
- **Elohim flattening**: Jerome flattened the elohim vocabulary (Psalm 82, Genesis 6) losing divine council meaning. Always restore original range.
- **"Church"**: Never use for ekklesia when precision matters. Ekklesia = governing assembly, political term.

## SECTION 3 — EVIDENCE TIER SYSTEM (MANDATORY ON ALL CONTENT)

Every claim in era chapter synopses, sections, and scholarly notes must be tagged with its evidence tier:

| Tag | Meaning | Usage |
|-----|---------|-------|
| **[A]** | Direct Scripture — canonical text states it explicitly | Reserved for explicit biblical statements |
| **[B]** | Valid inference — logically necessary from Scripture | Theologically derived conclusions |
| **[C]** | Ancient parallels — non-canonical texts or archaeological context illuminating the text | 1 Enoch, Jubilees, ANE background, archaeology |

**Rule**: Never assert a [C] claim as if it were [A]. Never mix tiers without labeling.

## SECTION 5 — THEOLOGICAL NON-NEGOTIABLES
### (Content that contradicts these = content corruption. Reject or flag immediately.)

### 5.1 — Divine Council
- **Psalm 82**: Real divine beings given governing authority over nations, judged for corruption. NOT a metaphor.
- **Deuteronomy 32:8-9 (DSS/LXX)**: Yahweh divided nations according to the number of the sons of God. Israel = Yahweh's direct portion.
- **Genesis 6:1-4**: Sons of God (b'nei ha-elohim) = divine/angelic beings = Watchers. NEVER Sethite view.
  - Sethite view (sons of God = righteous men of Seth's line) was invented by Julius Africanus, 3rd century AD
  - Has zero Hebrew grounding
  - The phrase b'nei ha-elohim appears 4 other times (Job 1:6, 2:1, 38:7, Psalm 29:1) — always divine beings
- **Azazel (Leviticus 16)**: Specific imprisoned Watcher — scapegoat ritual sends Israel's sins back to originator, not merely "into the wilderness"
- **Four categories of fallen beings** (never conflate):
  1. Imprisoned Watchers — Tartarus/Abyss
  2. Territorial Princes — corrupt sons of God over nations (Daniel 10)
  3. Demons — disembodied Nephilim spirits (1 Enoch 15:8-10)
  4. Satan/Nachash — distinct original rebel

### 5.2 — Pre-History
- **Satan's fall**: BEFORE Eden. Throne guardian cherub (Ezekiel 28:13-15). Job 38:7 places him unfallen at creation.
- **Eden was not the origin of evil** — it was Satan's first act of war against image-bearers
- **Three INDEPENDENT rebellions** (never conflate them):
  1. **Eden** — Actor: Nachash. Sin: corrupting human loyalty. Reversal: The Cross (Colossians 2:15)
  2. **Hermon** — Actor: 200 Watchers under Shemihazah (independent from Nachash). Reversal: 1 Peter 3:19 (kerysso = victory proclamation, NOT euangelizo = evangelism)
  3. **Babel** — Actor: Humanity under Nimrod + corrupt sons of God. Reversal: Pentecost (Acts 2 echoes LXX of Deuteronomy 32:8 and Genesis 11)
- **Nephilim**: Hebrew nəfilîm, root naphal = "fallen ones." LXX gigantes muddied etymology. Corruption extended to ALL flesh (Genesis 6:12, 1 Enoch 7:5).
- **Nimrod**: gibbor (Genesis 10:8) = same word as Nephilim offspring. Likely = Gilgamesh.
- **Pagan mythologies** = corrupted memories of real events involving real beings (Greek, Roman, Norse, Meso-American, Egyptian all traceable)

### 5.3 — Christology
- **Jesus = the Word**, present at creation (John 1:1-3 deliberately echoes Genesis 1:1)
- **Cloud-rider = exclusively divine prerogative**: Mark 14:62-63 — high priest tore robes over the cloud-rider claim, not the Messiah claim alone
- **Jesus' priesthood = Melchizedekian, NOT Levitical** (wrong tribe: Judah not Levi, Hebrews 7:13-14)
  - Malkhi-Tzedeq = "King of Righteousness" + "King of Peace" (tzedeq + salem)
  - No genealogy, no birth, no death recorded in Genesis 14 — deliberate silence
  - Psalm 110:4 = most quoted OT text in NT
- **1 Peter 3:19**: kerysso (κηρύσσω) = herald a victory proclamation. NOT preaching the gospel to give imprisoned spirits a second chance. Victory announcement to Watchers.
- **Genesis 3:15 = thesis of entire Bible**: The seed of the woman vs. the seed of the serpent war from Genesis 4 through Revelation 20.

### 5.4 — Salvation & Ekklesia
- **Salvation = covenantal**, not transactional. Never imply a "say this prayer and you're in" formula.
- **Thief on the cross = the floor** — minimum of covenantal trust, not the model.
- Works = evidence of salvation, not the basis.
- **Ekklesia (ἐκκλησία)** = governing assembly, NOT "church." Political term — assembly of citizens with authority.
  - Primary audience: cosmic powers (Ephesians 3:10)
  - Believers will judge angels (1 Corinthians 6:3) = Psalm 82 inverted — fallen sons of God replaced by redeemed humanity
- **Bema seat** (2 Corinthians 5:10) = reward evaluation, NOT salvation judgment. Olympic prize platform.
  - Five crowns, all cast before the throne (Revelation 4:10)
  - Luke 19 minas parable = clearest reward text: ten cities / five cities / nothing

### 5.5 — Afterlife
- **Sheol** = universal waiting place for the dead (righteous AND wicked). NOT hell, NOT heaven.
- **Death = sleep** (koimaomai). Hebrew nephesh = unified whole, not an immortal soul separable from body.
- **"Souls go to heaven" = Platonic infiltration** via Alexandria, codified by Augustine — NOT Hebrew.
- **Purgatory**: No textual basis. Requires Platonic soul. Contradicts Hebrews 10:14 and John 19:30 (tetelestai = paid in full).
- **Final hope = bodily resurrection on new earth** — God comes DOWN (Revelation 21:1-4). Eden fulfilled.
- **Immortality = gift at resurrection**, not inherent property of the soul.

### 5.6 — Eschatology
- **No rapture**. Darby origin (1830). Zero pre-1830 attestation in church history.
- **One visible return** — every eye will see it (Revelation 1:7).
- **Apantesis** (1 Thessalonians 4:17) = delegation going OUT to escort a dignitary back INTO the city = escort back to earth, not departure to heaven.
- **Eight-stage sequence**:
  1. Church Age
  2. Tribulation (requires rebuilt temple)
  3. Return of Christ (visible, physical, on clouds)
  4. First Resurrection
  5. Millennium (1,000-year reign on earth)
  6. Final Rebellion (Gog/Magog = POST-millennium, Revelation 20 — NOT current events)
  7. Great White Throne judgment
  8. New Creation (God comes DOWN)
- **Dispensationalism and Christian Zionism** = 19th century inventions. Not historical Christianity.
- **Modern Israel ≠ covenant Israel**. No ethnic distinction in new covenant (Galatians 3:28). Remnant future recognition = Zechariah 12:10.

### 5.7 — Prophets & Prayer
- **Prophets = covenant prosecutors** using the riv (ריב = lawsuit) framework. Royal messengers delivering Yahweh's case against covenant-breaking Israel.
- "Thus says Yahweh" = royal messenger formula. Not inspiration language — legal messenger language.
- Riv = 6-part Hittite treaty structure: call to witnesses → charge → history of relationship → indictment → sentence → call to return
- **Psalms and Prophets raw emotional texture MUST NEVER be sanitized**. The laments are nearly half the Psalter.
- **Prayer = palal (פלל)** = to intercede, to judge, to intervene actively. Not passive asking.
- Matthew 6:10 "Your kingdom come" = eltheto (imperative) = cause it to come. Active, not wishful.
- David = man after Yahweh's heart = orientation not perfection. Raw, broken, authentic.

### 5.8 — Holy Spirit & Gifts
- **Cessationism = not exegetically supported**.
- 1 Corinthians 13:10 — "when the perfect comes" = teleios = completion = the return of Christ, NOT the completed canon (no first-century reader would understand it that way).
- Gifts continue. Different communities carry different gifts.
- Spirit is active and moving today.

```

cross_refs      → Array: [{ ref, note, type }]
```

### 6.2 — Cross-Reference Type Tags (MANDATORY — unlabeled cross-refs = corruption)
- `ot` — Old Testament
- `nt` — New Testament
- `ane` — Ancient Near Eastern (non-biblical historical/archaeological)
- `dss` — Dead Sea Scrolls
- `pseudepigrapha` — 1 Enoch, Jubilees, etc.
- `thematic` — Thematic/topical connection

### 6.3 — Section Depth Standards
- Minimum: **3 sections** per chapter
- Maximum: **6 sections** per chapter
- Each section body: **800-1200 characters**
- Sections should build — context → detail → application/significance
- Never write sections shorter than 800 chars. Thin sections = content corruption.

### 6.4 — Flow Translation Files
```python
FLOW_{BOOK} = {
    chapter_number: {
        verse_number: "Flowing English prose...",
    }
}
NOTES_{BOOK} = {
    "chapter:verse": "Scholarly note referencing Hebrew/Greek...",
}
```
- Flow prose = readable, resonant English — not woodenly literal, not paraphrase
- Notes must reference Hebrew or Greek term by name when discussing meaning

### 6.5 — Glossary Entry Standards
