- Three reversals through Christ: Cross (Eden), Proclamation to spirits (Hermon), Pentecost (Babel)

### Nephilim Taxonomy (02)
- Matthew 22:30 "in heaven" is a qualifier, not universal prohibition
- Four distinct categories: Imprisoned Watchers, Territorial Princes, Demons/Nephilim spirits, Satan/Nachash
- Different sins, timing, punishment, current status for each

- Hebrews 1:6 quotes Deuteronomy 32:43 (LXX/DSS) — divine council commanded to worship the Son
- Ephesians 3:10 — the church IS the vehicle for announcing God's wisdom to cosmic powers (CERTAIN)
- 1 Cor 6:2-3 — divine council inversion: humans judge the very angels who failed them
- 1 Peter 3:19 — kerysso (victory announcement), not euangelizo (gospel preaching)
